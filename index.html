ABOUT US

Our Founder and CEO, Brzane Barmaja, spent much of his life operating restaurants all over the world. Through the years he developed a profound love for the pizza business and a passion for franchising. After having spent time living in Damascus, brought a Italian Style Pizza concept to the Land of Enchantment. He opened the first Pizza Stars restaurant on March 21, 2000, in Syria , Damscus. The following year they sold their first franchise and have continued selling Pizza Until it was the best pizza restaurnat in the middle east.
